St Nicholas is a well established Pre-school based in Southbourne, We have been running for over 40 years. We are an equal opportunity setting and welcome all children between the ages of 2 and 5 years old.

We follow the 'Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)' curriculum. We are Ofsted registered and can offer 15 hours free childcare for children aged 3 and 4.
